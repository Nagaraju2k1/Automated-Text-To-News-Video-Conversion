Automated Text-to-News Video Generation.

Extraction of Press Release: BeautifulSoup, Scrapy.
Categorization: Naive Bayes Classifier.
Summarization of Text: Using Gemini API.
Keywords generation: Spacy.
Images Collection (Open Source): Google_imagespy
Generating of audio: gtts
Generating Slideshow of images: MoviePy, OpenCV for image manipulation
Generation of Lip Sync video: Wav2Lip (Deep learning models trained on lip movements)
Integration of slideshow, audio, and Lip Sync video: MoviePy


In an age characterized by the fast pace of news consumption, our cutting-edge platform redefines conventional news stories as engaging video presentations. Our users can easily come to our site and select a news headline of their preference. The system identifies and aggregates key information automatically, converting long text-based news into engaging videos with synchronized visuals, clear voice, and accurate lip synchronization. To enhance user interaction and accessibility, captions are discreetly integrated, thus ensuring clarity for diverse audiences. Multilingual capabilities are enabled by our platform, delivering news content in four different languages, thus broadening its reach and impact. This groundbreaking solution revolutionizes the way audiences interact with news, making information consumption more interactive, accessible, and visually appealing.
